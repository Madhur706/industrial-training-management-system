About Industrial Training Management System

Industrial Training Management System is a web-based application developed using Django. This system helps students and administrators manage industrial training processes such as attendance management, training applications, reports, certificates, and student records efficiently and digitally.

Student Management

Manage student registration, profiles, and training records.

Attendance System

Track daily attendance and monitor training progress.

Certificate Management

Upload and download industrial training certificates easily.
